About Me

Hello! I'm Jahhaza Assiqooya. I was born and raised in Jember, East Java, a beautiful region known for its rich culture, delicious cuisine, and stunning natural landscapes.

As someone who loves my hometown, I created this website to showcase the beauty and uniqueness of Jember. From its famous tobacco plantations to its beautiful beaches like Papuma and Watu Ulo, Jember has so much to offer.

Thank you for visiting my website. I hope you enjoy learning about Jember as much as I enjoy sharing it with you!

Jember Nusantara

Jember is a regency located in East Java Province, Indonesia. Its capital is
Jember City, often referred to as the "City of Tobacco" due to its significant tobacco
plantations. This regency is situated in the eastern part of Java Island, in the Tapal Kuda region.
It shares its borders with Probolinggo Regency to the north, Lumajang Regency to the west,
the Indian Ocean to the south, and Bondowoso Regency to the east. Jember is known for its
fertile agricultural land, beautiful beaches, and vibrant cultural festivals.

Pecel Pincuk

Traditional rice dish served on banana leaves with fresh vegetables, rich peanut sauce, and various side dishes. A authentic Jember culinary delight.

Soto Jember

Signature chicken soup with clear aromatic broth, served with rice, bean sprouts, and fried shallots. Comfort food that warms the heart.

Tape Jember

Fermented cassava snack, sweet with slight alcoholic taste. The most famous souvenir from Jember, perfect as gift for family and friends.

Sego Tempong

Extremely spicy rice dish with various side dishes. For those who dare to challenge their taste buds with Jember's legendary spiciness.

Kripik Tempe Jember

Kripik Tempe Jember is one of the typical souvenirs made from thinly sliced tempeh that is fried until crispy. With a crunchy flour coating.

Pia Edamame Jember

Pia Edamame Jember is a unique and innovative souvenir made from fresh edamame beans grown in Jember's fertile highlands.

Jenang Waluh Jember

Jenang Waluh Jember is a traditional pumpkin pudding that showcases the agricultural richness of Jember's pumpkin harvest. This sweet, dense confection is made from locally grown pumpkins cooked.

Prol Tape

Traditional cake made from fermented cassava (tape) with soft texture and sweet taste. Unique Jember souvenir that combines tape with cake innovation.

Tourist Destinations

Papuma Beach

Papuma Beach is one of Jember's most famous tourist destinations, known for its white sand and unique rock formations. The name "Papuma" is an abbreviation of "Pasir Putih Malikan," which means Malikan White Sand.

Location
Jember Mini Zoo

Jember Mini Zoo is a family-friendly attraction featuring various animal species from Indonesia and around the world. It offers interactive experiences, animal shows, and educational programs.

Location
Watu Ulo Beach

Watu Ulo Beach is known for its dramatic coastline and the snake-like rock formation that gives the beach its name ("Watu Ulo" means "Snake Rock" in Javanese). The powerful waves and scenic views.

Location
Teluk Love

Teluk Love (Love Bay) is a beautiful hidden beach in Jember with a unique heart-shaped bay. This picturesque spot offers stunning coastal views, clear blue waters, and perfect sunset moments.

Location
Jember Fashion Carnaval

The Jember Fashion Carnaval is an internationally renowned fashion event held annually. It features extravagant costumes, creative designs, and vibrant parades that attract visitors from around the world.

Location
Tancak Waterfall

Tancak Waterfall is a stunning natural attraction located in Jember, East Java. Surrounded by lush green forests, this waterfall offers a refreshing escape with its cool waters and scenic beauty.
